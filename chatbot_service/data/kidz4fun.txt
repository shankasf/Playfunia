# PlayFunia Business Information
## 1. Company Overview
*   **Name:** PlayFunia
*   **Mission/Values:**
    *   FUN & ACTIVE PLAY: Playground inspires movement, laughter, and imagination.
    *   BIRTH DAY PARTY: Simple and stress-free parties with themed setups and friendly hosts.
    *   SAFE & CLEAN SPACE: Cleanliness and safety are top priority for parents to relax while kids explore.
*   **Target Age Group:** 1 to 13 years old.
*   **General Admission:** Unlimited play for the day. Must sign a waiver and bring or purchase grip socks.

## 5. Facility Offerings
*   **Trampoline:** Soft, safe, and fun.

*   **Ball Pool:** Large ball pit for sensory adventure.
*   **Private Party Room:** Colorful, cozy room for birthdays and celebrations.

## 6. Policies and Terms (from Waiver and FAQs)
*   **Waiver:** Participant Waiver, Release, Assumption of Risk, and Indemnification Agreement is required for use of facilities.
*   **Decorations:** Customers are welcome to bring their own decorations for private party rooms, but **confetti or glitter are not allowed**.
*   **Rescheduling:** Contact at least 48 hours in advance to reschedule a party.
*   **Grip Socks:** Required for play.
*   **Food/Drink:** Outside food is generally allowed only with the MINI FUN party package, or as specified in other packages.
