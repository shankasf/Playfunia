# Playfunia Chatbot Knowledge Base

## Business Overview
Playfunia is a premier indoor playground and event venue designed for children aged 1 to 13. Our mission is to provide a safe, clean, and vibrant environment where kids can explore their imagination through active play and movement.

## Location and Contact Information
Playfunia is located in Albany, NY, at 1 Crossgates Mall Rd, Unit N202, on Level 2 near Macy's. We are open daily from 9:00 AM to 7:00 PM. You can reach us by phone at +1 (201) 539-5928 or via email at info@Playfunia.com. We are also active on Facebook, Instagram, and TikTok.

## Facility Attractions
Our facility features several exciting attractions:
- Trampolines: Soft and safe for high-energy bouncing.
- Slides: Colorful structures that are a favorite for all visitors.
- Ball Pools: Large sensory adventures filled with bright balls.
- Private Party Rooms: Cozy spaces dedicated to hosting birthday celebrations.

## Admission and Pricing
General admission is $20.00, which includes entry for one child and one adult for unlimited daily play. A sibling discount is available for $35.00 for two siblings. Additional guests, such as extra adults or non-playing guests, are charged a fee of $5.00. All participants are required to wear grip socks (D-Socks), which can be purchased on-site for $3.00. Digital gift cards are also available ranging from $20.00 to $120.00. Please note that all visitors must sign a waiver on the day of their visit.

## Birthday Party Packages
We offer three tiers of party packages, all including admission for up to 10 children and one adult per child. Additional children are $40 each, and extra guests are $10 each.
- Mini Fun ($399): Includes 2-hour private room access and all-day park access. Guests provide their own snacks and decorations.
- Super Fun ($599): Includes everything in Mini Fun plus party supplies, pizza, drinks, and snack trays.
- Mega Fun ($699): Includes everything in Super Fun plus custom themed balloons and matching tableware.

## Policies and FAQs
Safety and cleanliness are our top priorities. We follow strict sanitization protocols and provide staff supervision. Parties can be rescheduled with at least 48 hours' notice. While you are welcome to bring your own decorations for private rooms, please do not use confetti or glitter. For more detailed information on refunds and guest conduct, please refer to our full policies on the website.
